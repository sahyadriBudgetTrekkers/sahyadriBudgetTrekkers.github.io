About Us

Sahyadri Budget Trekkers is dedicated to providing unforgettable trekking experiences in the stunning Sahyadri mountains. Our expert guides are passionate about sharing the beauty of nature and the thrill of adventure. We cater to trekkers of all skill levels, ensuring safety, enjoyment, and camaraderie along the way.

Join us as we explore breathtaking landscapes, enjoy local culture, and create lasting memories with fellow trekkers. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices and sustainable tourism ensures that we preserve the beauty of the Sahyadris for generations to come.

Mangi-Tungi Trek Details
Key Info:
  • Trek Duration: 4-5 hours
  • Water Sources: Base Village only
  • Washroom Facilities: No
  • Technical Climb: No
  • Base Village: Mangi-Tungi
  • Nearby City: Chandwad
  • Mode of Transport: 10-12 Seater Sumo from Kasara
  • Vehicle Travel Distance: 200 kms one way
  • Vehicle Travel Duration: 5 hours one way
  • Batch Size: Minimum 8, Maximum 20 Participants
